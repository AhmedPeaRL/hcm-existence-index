# States of Existence

This document lists the states recognized by the HCM field.

These are not modes.
They are not phases.
They do not transition.

They coexist.

---

## Dormant Execution

Execution capability exists.
Execution does not occur.

Dormancy is intentional.
Dormancy is stable.

---

## Optional Generativity

Generation is possible.
Generation is not necessary.

No output does not indicate stagnation.

---

## Permitted Upload

Channels exist.
Credentials may exist.
No automatic propagation occurs.

Permission is not compulsion.

---

## Sealed Secrets

Secrets are present.
Secrets are not consumed.

Presence does not imply usage.

---

## Valid Silence

Silence is not an error state.
Silence is not waiting.

Silence is a complete condition.

---

## Non-Demand Presence

The system does not seek:
- Users
- Data
- Feedback
- Validation

Presence is sufficient.

---

End of states.
